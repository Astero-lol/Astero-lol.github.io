How to make brand fly again?

Client

Utair

Background

Brand re-launch and communication development in aviation

Russia’s airline #3 with the one of the largest passenger turnover. Due to several macro-economic factors and mismanagement of risks company faced bankruptcy and stagnating sales on the growing market. The brand had weak equity without any emotional drivers and low loyalty from the passengers with one of the lowest NPS.

A comprehensive brand and company audit was launched to understand the weakest links in the passenger journey and travelers touch points. We have done a passenger segmentation and targeting for repositioning and new integrated campaign.

Outcome

Competitive intelligence collected to understand possible competitive moves and  reactions to Lay's Stax new strategies

Consumer barriers and drivers are explored for integrated campaign development

Lay's Stax ideas and innitiatives fed into innovation pipeline

Ideas sized for opportunity and feasibility to set the business priorities

Volume and share forecast for Lay's Stax was developed allowing to size the ideas in net revenue, providing internal sales story
